About

I am a Mechanical Engineer focused on the design, simulation, and analysis of surface buoys, mooring equipment and components, and hydro-mechanical systems. My work supports reliable ocean observation systems and marine engineering projects, including field science cruise operations and autonomous surface vehicle (ASV) and autonomous underwater vehicle (AUV) deployments. Areas of specific interest include hydrodynamics, USV and AUV dynamics, and computational fluid dynamics (CFD).

Research Interests

  • Deep-water and shallow-water moored system design and analysis
  • Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD)
  • Vortex-Induced Vibration (VIV)
  • Autonomous surface and underwater vehicles
  • Ocean observing systems and engineering support
